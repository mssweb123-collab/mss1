Our Curriculum
The Curriculum is designed to develop the total personality of the child laying equal emphasis on academic as well as physical and creative activities. MSS MATRICULATION School strictly follows all the norms and patterns advised by the Government of Tamil Nadu in all the scholastic and non scholastic activities. Art, Craft, Physical training, Computer skills, indoor/outdoor games are for all students. Field trips, Club activities and workshops are a regular feature. Social service visit are also organized. The medium of instruction is English. Hindi and Malayalam will also be included in the curriculum. Periodical and monthly evaluation and Parent/Teachers meeting will help to assess the level of attainment of the students. Worksheets are also given to assess the learning process. We are conducting special classes for slow learners in all Saturdays.

About MSS School
M.S.S. School is situated in the village of Uppatty, Pandalur block, The Nilgiris district, Tamil Nadu state, India. M.S.S. Matriculation was started as English medium nursery and primary school. The school became Matriculation in the year 2000.

We occupy the children to enjoy the fruit of learning and explore the art, sport and co-curricular talents in them.
Each student is encouraged and motivated by the desire to meet the goal in their life.
The core value of our institution lies in the end result and not in exam result.
The educational development and all around growth (including co-curricular
and extracurricular activities) of students is being taken care. There by assuring the students passed out from our school will be capable to face the challenges in the outer world.
