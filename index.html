About Us

Nurturing Young Minds Since 1992

MSS Matriculation School is situated in the serene village of Uppatty, Pandalur, The Nilgiris. Started as an English medium nursery school, it became a full Matriculation school in the year 2000.

Run by MSS Trust (Minority Service Society) under the auspices of Uppatty Juma Masjid Committee, we provide student-centric education developing total personality of every child.
